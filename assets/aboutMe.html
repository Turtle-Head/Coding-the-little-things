A little bit about me

I have been working on and with computers since the age of 10. My first experience was at school (grade 3), where I learned how to make simple programs using Apple's Hypercard. It sparked a huge desire to get into what is now a much more sophisticated and involved way of generating content on computers.
I enrolled in the Front End Nanodegree program at Udacity and spent 6 months learning the intricaies of HTML5, CSS and JavaScript, and can finally say I have graduated. I can't say it wasn't a struggle at times to get things to work, trial and error were my best friends and still are, but I believe that this is a great way to learn. If you are looking for a great place to learn how to code check them out.
When I am not at my computer you can usually find me with a book in hand somewhere quiet enjoying myself, or to listening to music. If you want to reach me for a project or something else go ahead and send me an email.

Happy coding!
